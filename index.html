About ICTSM

Mission & Vision

Information Communication Technology Systems & Maintenance (ICTSM) is a vocational trade focused on equipping students with the practical skills needed to install, maintain, and troubleshoot IT systems and networks. Students learn hardware repair, OS installation, LAN/WAN basics, electrical fundamentals for IT setups, preventive maintenance, and basic security practices. Our mission is to make graduates job-ready for roles in IT support, network maintenance, and entry-level engineering positions.

Who should enroll?

Students who like hands-on work, problem solving, and want entry-level IT roles.

Career outcomes

IT Technician, Field Support Engineer, Network Installer, System Maintenance Technician, Helpdesk Engineer.
